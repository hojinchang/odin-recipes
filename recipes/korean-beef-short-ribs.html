Kalbi (Korean BBQ Beef Short Ribs)

Description

Kalbi is a staple of Korean fine dining but too expensive in restaurants. It is simple to make at home and will leave your quests asking for more. Total time includes marinating time. It's traditionally served sliced, rolled in romaine lettuce leaves with white rice, lightly browned garlic slices, and gochujang (Korean red pepper paste).

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup soy sauce
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Asian (toasted) sesame oil
  • 2 pounds Korean-style short ribs

Directions

  1. Stir together soy sauce, brown sugar, water, green, onions, garlic, and sesame oil in a bowl until sugar has dissolved.
  2. Place ribs in a large resealable plastic bag. Pour marinade over ribs. Seal the bag and place in refrigerator. Let marinate for 3 hours to overnight.
  3. Preheat outdoor grill to medium-high heat. Lightly oil the grate.
  4. Remove ribs from the bag; shake off excess maridnate. Grill ribs on the pre-heated grill for 5 to 7 minutes per side.
